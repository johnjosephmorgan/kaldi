African Accented French Corpus

This corpus consists of approximately 22 hours of speech recordings.
Transcripts are provided for All the recordings.
It can be divided into 3 parts:

1. Yaounde
Collected by USMA's CTELL in 2003 in Yaounde Cameroon.
It has 84 speakers, 48 male and 36 female.

2. CA16
This part was collected by a team sponsored by DARPA's Boyan Yaneskovich in 2016.
It has 125 speakers from Cameroon, Chad, Congo and Gabon.

3. Niger
This part was collected from 23 speakers in 2015.
